Basement Remodeling in West Chester PA

Falgiatore Construction, LLC is one of the top Basement Remodelers in the West Chester PA (19382 & 19380) area. We are Basement Remodeling Contractors that have been providing basement finishing for homeowners in the West Chester PA area since 2000.

Basement remodeling really transforms your living space. Whether it is a simple basement design or you desire features such as a bathroom, wet bar, exercise room, mounted TV's, etc., Falgiatore Construction, LLC can help!

Basement Remodeling is a Smart Investment

Adding more living space means that there are more options available to you as your family grows.

Turn Your Basement into an Entertainment Room

Transforming your basement into an entertainment room is an excellent way to make good use of that space. You can do anything from a simple TV room to a tech savvy entertainment room. You can even add a pool table or ping pong to create a game room for the kids.

Create a Man Cave in Your Basement

One popular reason to finish a basement is to create the quintessential man-cave. These are usually stocked with a big-screen TV and comfy couches, a bar, and even a pool table. This can help maintain marital harmony by keeping all the guy stuff out of the main part of the house!

Set Up Your Basement as an Office

Technology has allowed more and more people to work from home, whether it's via telecommuting or running their own business. A finished and renovated basement offers a perfect space for a home office, and the renovation can ensure all necessary wiring and lights are installed.

Use Your Basement as a Wine Cellar

If you're into fine wines, you may be sitting on the perfect storage space. Many wines that age well require consistent temperatures with low light exposure, such as the cool dark of most basements. A skilled renovation can easily change a basement into a splendid wine cellar.
